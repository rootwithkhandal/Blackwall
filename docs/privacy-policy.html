Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Local First Privacy

BlackWall is an open-source project designed to run locally on your infrastructure. All packet inspection, machine learning inference, and blockchain ledger hashing occurs strictly on your device.

External Integrations

If configured, BlackWall may send IP addresses to third-party Threat Intelligence providers (Shodan, AbuseIPDB, VirusTotal) to enrich security logs. Threat detection data (verdicts and IPs) may also be forwarded to your configured SIEM tools (Splunk, Wazuh) or Webhooks (Discord, Slack) if you provide the API keys.

Data Collection

We, the developers of BlackWall, do not track, collect, or store any of your network traffic or firewall decisions. You retain 100% ownership and control over your security data.
